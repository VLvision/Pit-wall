OpenF1 Live Access
Free OpenF1 data is blocked during live sessions (30 min before to 30 min after). Paste a live access token below to unlock live timing. Without a token, PitWall works fully for finished sessions.
How to get a token
  1. Subscribe at openf1.org (Sponsor button)
  2. Get your username & password
  3. Tap Fetch Token below and enter them — PitWall gets a 1-hour token for you
Your login is sent once to OpenF1 to get a token, then discarded — it's never saved in the app.
